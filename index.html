Hon'ble Education Advisor

As the Principal of Cumilla Polytechnic Institute, Engr. Muhammad Majharul Islam Chowdhury believes that true education goes beyond textbooks.Taking effective steps after reviewing recommendations from various education commissions. Vision for the Future: Dr. Abrar has expressed his dream of an education system where students can envision their future within Bangladesh and, in time, lead the world from the country. He sees education as the most important vehicle for building an equitable society. Teacher Importance and Support: He has stressed the importance of improving the living standards of teachers, noting that the increase in house rent allowance for Monthly Pay Order (MPO)-listed teachers is a testament to the state's responsibility toward those who shape the nation's future. He calls teaching a noble profession. Digital Transformation: The digitalization process is ongoing at every level of the education system to improve standards through initiatives like online transfers, e-filing, and edutech..
